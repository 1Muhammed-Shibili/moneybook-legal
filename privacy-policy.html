Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

MoneyBook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Age Restriction: MoneyBook is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use MoneyBook, we may collect the following information that you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (if provided via Google Sign-In)
  • Financial Data: Income records, expense records, transaction details, categories, notes, and amounts (all stored on your behalf)
  • Currency Preferences: Your selected currency for financial tracking
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, session duration
  • Network Information: Internet connectivity status

2.3 Google Sign-In Data

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Google Account email address
  • Google Account profile name
  • Google Account unique identifier (User ID)
  • Authentication tokens to maintain your session

We do not have access to your Google Account password. Google Sign-In is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To create and manage your account, store your financial data, and provide core app functionality
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain secure access to your account
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on your preferences (e.g., currency selection)
  • Sync Across Devices: To synchronize your data across multiple devices using cloud storage
  • Improve Our Services: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

4.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

MoneyBook uses Google Firebase services to provide core functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication: For user authentication and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: For secure storage and synchronization of your financial data
  • Firebase Hosting: For app configuration and content delivery

Data Shared with Firebase: Email address, user ID, authentication tokens, financial transaction data, categories, and user settings.

Important: Firebase is a service provided by Google LLC. Your data stored in Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and security measures. Data is stored on Google's secure servers and may be processed in countries where Google operates data centers.

For more information, visit: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Sign-In

When you use Google Sign-In, Google may collect certain information about your use of the App. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Future Payment Processing (RevenueCat/Google Play Billing)

When subscription features are added, we may use:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • RevenueCat (optional): For subscription management and analytics

Payment information (credit card details) will be processed directly by Google Play Store and will not be stored on our servers.

4.4 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Cloud Storage: Your financial data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore databases
  • Local Storage: Some preferences may be cached locally on your device for performance
  • Geographic Location: Data may be stored and processed in any country where Google operates Firebase infrastructure

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Data encryption at rest in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Security Rules to prevent unauthorized access
  • Secure authentication using Firebase Auth and Google Sign-In
  • Regular security audits and updates

Important: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup Data: Backups containing your data may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

6.2 Your Right to Delete Your Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Via App: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Via Email: Contact us at 1muhammedshibili@gmail.com

Upon receiving a deletion request, we will:

  1. Verify your identity
  2. Permanently delete your account and all associated financial data
  3. Send you a confirmation email within 7 business days
  4. Complete the deletion process within 30 days

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please export your data before deleting your account if you wish to keep a copy.
